# Explorating-Data-Analytics-EDA-in-Hospitality-Domain-For-Atliq-Hotel-Python-


Problem Statement: 
Atliq Grands, a well-established entity in India's hotel industry, is facing increased competition, a decline in revenue, and a loss of market share. They sought to leverage data and strategic insights to understand and address these challenges. The objective was to analyze the situation, provide actionable insights, and devise effective strategies for customer retention and revenue growth.

Project Goal: 
The goal was to conduct a comprehensive analysis of the hotel industry domain using Python. By leveraging data-driven insights and strategic recommendations, we aimed to empower Atliq Grands to make informed decisions. The ultimate aim was to assist Atliq Grands in regaining a competitive edge in the market, effectively addressing their challenges of revenue decline and market share loss.

Project Highlights: 
Data Cleaning:
Handling missing values and outliers.

 Data Transformation:
Added occupancy percentage columns.

 Insights Generation: Key questions answered include:
Calculating average occupancy rate by room type and city.
Identifying occupancy rate on weekdays and weekends.
Analyzing revenue generated per city.
Analyzing monthly revenue trends.
Assessing revenue by booking platforms.

Skills Learned: 
 Python Programming:
Enhanced proficiency in Python for data analysis, cleaning, transformation, and visualization.

 Data Visualization:
Leveraged Matplotlib library for creating charts and bars.

 Pandas Fundamentals:
Creating various DataFrames.
Group by functions.
Concatenation and merging of DataFrames.
Handling NA values.
Reading CSV files.
